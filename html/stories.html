The Alphan Crusade

The Alpha Phi Beta Fraternity is a College of Law-based fraternity in the University of the Philippines. To this date, the Alpha Phi Beta Fraternity maintains its singular existence in UP College of Law, with no recognized chapters outside the UP Diliman. Its present roster of Lords hails not only from the UP College of Law but also from the other colleges in the University.

Analysis on the Philippine Economy and Fiscal Policy

October 3, 2020


The Alphan Thought Leaders Series is a 4-week webinar series on key fields of study relevant to addressing the effects and issues brought by the COVID-19 pandemic. These topics are economics and taxation, the practice of law, freedom of expression, and nationalism and civic involvement. The goal of the webinar series is to provide much needed insights on how professionals in these fields can adapt to the new normal during and after the pandemic.
